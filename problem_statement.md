## Problem Statement

Atliq Solutions operates across multiple regions in India and sells computer hardware products to various clients.

The Sales Director faces challenges due to:
- Rapidly changing market conditions
- Lack of reliable, data-driven insights
- Dependence on verbal updates from regional managers
- Difficulty consolidating multiple Excel reports

The objective is to build a centralized dashboard that delivers accurate, real-time sales insights to support data-driven decision-making.
